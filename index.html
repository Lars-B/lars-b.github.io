Welcome to my website!

Thanks for stopping by. Take a look around and learn more about me and what I do.

I develop methods and software in the area of phylogenetics.
My work has focused on understanding distributions of phylogenetic time trees.
A major goal of this work is to improve the way phylogenetic inference of trees is performed,
particularly in the context of BEAST2 but applicable to all tree inference software.
